### 1. Why this proxy is called Griffin?

This proxy combines two different communication models,
gRPC-Web and gRPC, into a single unified system.
Its hybrid nature makes Griffin a fitting name.

### 2. What projects inspired Griffin?

Griffin draws inspiration from several excellent open-source projects:

- [Grpc Web](https://github.com/improbable-eng/grpc-web)
- [Tonic](https://github.com/hyperium/tonic)

### 3. What specifications does Griffin follow?

Griffin adheres to the official gRPC and HTTP/2 specifications.

- <https://github.com/grpc/grpc/blob/master/doc/PROTOCOL-HTTP2.md>
- <https://github.com/grpc/grpc/blob/master/doc/PROTOCOL-WEB.md>
- <https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/rfc7540/>
